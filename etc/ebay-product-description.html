Product Description

A lightweight unit of protection that snugly guards your phone. Legionís deceptively slim profile packs durable double layers, encasing your devices with certified military grade protection. Flexible TPU provides shock absorption, while a rigid outer shell of Polycarbonate armors your phone from impacts. Legion embodies strength and security--your phoneís personal bodyguard.
