Times may be tough, but so are we. Protection has always been our top priority, and now, we are putting our best effort towards creating a safe environment for everyone, including you. We recognize that we are stronger as a team, and it is our goal to provide help and support to those in our community. Here is how we’re stepping up.

How we're protecting

Working from home

We’re still here for you, not from our offices but from our homes. We’re staying safe due to California’s stay-at-home mandate. We are still here to answer any questions you may have so don’t hesitate to reach out.

Safe and clean shipping

At our warehouse in California, we’re taking extra precautions such as providing masks and gloves daily, and providing hand sanitizing stations at a safe distance. This ensures the safety of you and our team, while all packages are safely shipped within minimal processing time.

Worry-free returns

No need to fret because we got you. From March 1, 2020 to May 31, 2020, all orders will be eligible for 60-day returns. We want to give you a smooth shopping experience, with zero hassle.

100% Proceeds for Covid-19 Relief

We’re partnering with Direct Relief to aid healthcare workers with medical resources globally. Join us in bringing relief to brave workers fighting on the front lines. Let’s make a difference together.

Giving with purpose

You are a part of this movement. Each purchase made will go towards providing materials and supplies to those leading the battle against Covid-19. All proceeds of each item ordered through Spigen Branded Webstores from April 9th to the 30th will go directly towards purchasing and distributing equipment to medical personnel, primarily in hospitals and urgent care centers.

Join Direct Relief

Healthcare workers are on the front lines, often with minimal protective gear to keep themselves safe. Our partnership allows us to directly help our healthcare heroes as they work tirelessly to protect us. We’ve set a goal to reach $50,000 in donations to support relief efforts for Covid-19. So join us and together we will make a difference.
