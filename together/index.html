Times may be tough, but so are we. We recognize that we are stronger as a team, and it is our goal to provide help and support to those in our community.

Protection has always been our top priority, and now, we are putting our best effort towards creating a safe environment for everyone, including you. Here is how we're stepping up.

How we're protecting

Work from home

All our employees are staying safe and working remotely in accordance with California’s Safer-at-home measures. We’re still a tight-knit team, ensuring that we can continue to be here for any questions or concerns you may have.

Safe shipping

To ensure the safety of our team, we are taking extra precautions with protective gear, frequent hand washing, social distancing, and use of hand sanitizing stations in our California warehouse. All packages are safely packaged and shipped with minimal processing time.

Worry-free return

Enjoy shopping stress-free with an extended window for returns. All orders placed between March 1, 2020, and May 31, 2020 will be eligible for 60-day returns. Feel free to return at your convenience, hassle-free.

Giving it all

From April 9th to May 31st, we are donating all proceeds from www.spigen.com . Join us in our goal to donate a total of $50,000 to Direct Relief, a charity that aids healthcare workers with medical resources both at home and abroad.

Join Direct Relief

We want to do our part to protect those fighting in the front lines. Our partnership with Direct Relief allows us to directly help our healthcare heroes as they work tirelessly to protect us. Big shoutout to all the HCWs out there!
